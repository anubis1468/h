# SurgeCoin Hybrid Ecosystem — Investor One-Pager

## Overview
SurgeCoin is the world’s first dual-mining crypto gaming ecosystem, combining virtual-world mining with real GPU-powered mining in a unified, player-owned economy.

## Problem
- Mining is inaccessible to non-technical users.
- Gaming economies lack real, liquid value.
- Miners and gamers operate in separate silos.

## Solution
A hybrid ecosystem where:
- Gamers earn through in-game mining.
- Miners earn through GPU mining.
- Both feed into a shared token economy.

## Tokens
- SurgeCoin (SCN): In-game and marketplace currency.
- SurgeCoin-GPU (SCN-G): GPU-mined token, convertible to SCN.

## Revenue Streams
- Marketplace fees
- Cosmetic and booster sales
- Tournament passes
- Staking spreads
- Partner integrations

## Roadmap Highlights
- 2026: Community, miner beta, marketplace, hybrid tournaments
- 2027: Token launch, staking, governance, mobile app, cross-chain bridge

## Why SurgeCoin
- First-mover in dual-mining gaming
- Strong alignment with Web3, gaming, and mining communities
- Scalable architecture and clear monetization
